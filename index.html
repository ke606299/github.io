02. About Me

Hi我是柯琼虹，来自广东汕头。我是一个会设计会剪辑会代码的即将"秃头"的99年学生。我是一个慢热的人，可以很安静，也可以很嬉闹。我喜欢隐藏负能量的情绪，因为不喜欢把不好的情绪带给别人，不想身边的人成为我的负能量垃圾桶。我无法带着坏情绪好好聊天，聊天会成为敷衍的聊天，别人也会因此感受到不开心。所以大多情绪不好的时候，很少加入聊天。认识我的人，大多第一感觉是我很高冷，认识后都知道我是一个好相处的人。我也是一个追求新鲜的人,不喜欢因循守旧，我喜欢探索新的乐趣。我还是一个“吹毛求疵”的人，因为我很严谨，也算是追求精细吧。我喜欢反复查看自己的工作产出，仔细检查有没有可以改进的点，希望可以做得更好。有时候为了制作出我喜欢的作品，我甚至可以通宵到早上五六点。我很喜欢一句话,你遇到的每个人都在经历着你理解不了的战斗，永远心存善意。我会继续保持善意的。

Hi, I'm Ke Qionghong from shantou, guangdong. I am a "bald" student of '99 who can design, clip and code. I am a slow heat, I can be quiet and playful. I like to hide negative emotions, because I don't like to bring bad emotions to others, and I don't want people around me to be my negative energy dustbin. I can't carry on a conversation with a negative emotion. The conversation becomes perfunctory and others feel unhappy because of it. So when I am in a bad mood, I seldom join the chat. Most people who know me feel that I am very indifferent at first, and they all know that I am a good person to get along with. I am also a person who pursues novelty. I don't like to follow the beaten path. I like to explore new pleasures. I am also a "picky" person, because I am very rigorous, also the pursuit of refinement. I like to check my work output again and again to see if there is anything I can improve and hope to do better. Sometimes I stay up until 5 or 6 in the morning to make something I like. I love the idea that everyone you meet is fighting a battle you don't understand and always has a heart of gold. I will continue to be kind.
